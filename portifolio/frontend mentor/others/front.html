Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal.This classic omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of chesese,vegetables or meat.

Preparation time

  1. Total: Approximately 10 minute
  2. Preparation:5 minute
  3. Cooking:5 minute

Ingredient

  1. 2-3 large eggs
  2. Salt,to taste
  3. Pepper,to taste
  4. 1 table spoon of butter or oil
  5. Optional fillings:cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

Instruction

  • Beat the eggs:in a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed,you can add a table spoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture

  • Heat the pan:place a non stick pan over a medium heat and add butter or oil

  • Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.

  • Add fillings(optional) When the eggs begins to set at edges but are still slightly runny in the middle,sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the Omelette

  • Fold and serve As the omelette continue to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings.Let it cook for another minute,and the slide it into plate

  • EnjoyServe hot,with additional salt and pepper if needed
